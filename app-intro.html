Apply for a small business loan.

This form helps you complete a pre-application for a loan supported by the SBA. This pre-application process should take around 15 minutes.

The process of this application includes 4 steps and requires particular information. You may want to gather this material before starting.

This form is the first phase of a two-part process. The full process is outlined below.

When you complete the application, you will be presented with the options that the SBA has determined are best suited for your needs. This may include a variety of SBA partners that you can compare and contact.

Phase One: General Information

This first phase collects your general information and reasoning for applying for a loan, and suggests potential loan offerings to suit your needs.

  1. Collect Business Details

    • Business name, classification, and industry
    • Business's registered zip code
    • How long you have been in business

  2. Collect Business Financials

    • Last year's annual revenue and net profit
    • Do you have collateral?
    • Are you a seasonal business?

  3. Collect Personal Details

    • Business owner's name and applicant's name, if different
    • Social security number and birthday
    • Veteran status

  4. Collect Funding Needs

    • Why you need funding
    • Amount of money requested
    • When you need the money

Phase Two: Match with a Lender

The second phase is a direct match with a lender. Your information will be forwarded to the partner institution. That partner institution will have its own ways of working with you, including potentially another website and the request for additional materials. After you choose a lender, your direct engagement with the SBA will be complete. The SBA will continue to back this loan, but will not be your main point of contact.
