You are eligible for this program if your business...

  • Is a small business as defined by the SBA industry guidelines
  • Is registered in the US as a sole proprietorship, LLC, Corporation, or B-Corp. Nonprofits and Trusts are not currently supported at this time.
  • You are looking for funding under $100,000.
  • You are looking for funding for: working capital, equipment, payroll, starting a business, or disaster funds.
  • While having a bankruptcy or poor credit may limit what we can offer, they currently do not exclude you from this program!

You are ineligible for this program if your business...

  • Firms engaged in activities that are prohibited by federal law or applicable law in the jurisdiction where the business is located or conducted.
  • Businesses in the following industries are currently not eligible:
    • Corporate-owned franchises
    • Pay day loan stores
    • Pawn shops
    • Astrology, palm reading
    • Adult bookstores, strip clubs
    • Track waging facilities
